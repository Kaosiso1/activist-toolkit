Take Action

The relentless degradation and unjust treatment on black women and girls have been occuring since the times of slavery. And it's sad to say things have not changed, America shifted its form of expolitation of African American women from slavery, to films, social media, and everday life. We all remember those events in middle/elementary school where a black girl was bullied for having kinky hair and ebony skin, and if you were one of those people to insult her appearance, shame on you! Those same innocent, little girls grow up to have self-hate issues and take on the mindset that they belong in this "angry, strong, warrior" trope and hide our vulnerability, because apparently it's all their good for. Malcolm X said, “The most disrespected woman in America, is the black woman. The most unprotected person in America is the black woman. The most neglected person in America, is the black woman.” He quoted this in 1962, it's 2020. Let's take the steps to discredit it. 🖤
